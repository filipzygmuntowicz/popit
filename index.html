What is Pop it exacly?

This is a child's game, in which the process can be compared to the bursting of a bubble wrap. But unlike film, pop-it can be pressed with the same characteristic sensations and sounds "endlessly": dozens of silocone bubbles are simply squeezed out in the other direction

Rules of the game

Players take turns and click on the bumps. It is imperative to finish the row that you started. You can press a maximum of 3 bubbles at the same times (they must be near, you cannot press several pimples at once, if they are not close to each other) The task is to make the oopponent go last. Who went last - he lost!)
